About

Emma Haslam, from Utah, and originally born in Murray, Utah, is someone who finds joy in planning fun trips and helping others. She values teamwork, enjoys coming up with new ideas, and cherishes making memories with friends and family. As a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, Emma's faith is an important part of her life, guiding her in her efforts to support and connect with those around her..

In addition, Emma loves exploring new places and experiences, always looking for ways to bring people together. Emma strives to have enthusiasm for life and to be mindful of other to help connect with people in meaningful ways.
